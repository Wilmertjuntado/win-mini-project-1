My Work-Flow

01 / Strategy & Research

The first step in any project is to get a better understanding of the client. The value in doing this work before moving a single pixel is to establish a greater sense of trust and to show each client that I care about their business and long term success.

02 / UX Design & Interaction Design

The architecture of a system is equally, if not more, important than how it looks and works. I leverage design-thinking in every project to get to the best human centered solution to a challenge with data. I design experiences for the user, not for myself.

03 / Ui Design & Prototyping

While beautiful ui designs can be subjective depending on the viewer, I enjoy the challenge of creating universally beautiful designs that also leverage micro-interactions. With visual design and prototyping, I get a chance to delight the user aesthetics and motion.

04 / User Testing & Iteration

Creating something and putting it out into the world is very rewarding. However, creating, aggregating feedback, synthesizing it in a way that makes sense and iterating based on user needs is much more fulfilling. Learning to measure progress and iterate is important.
